Community Guidelines

Last Updated: November 2025

Welcome to ShipMates! Our community guidelines help ensure a safe, respectful, and enjoyable experience for all cruisers.

Be Respectful

Treat all members with courtesy and respect. Harassment, hate speech, discrimination, or bullying of any kind will not be tolerated.

Keep It Appropriate

ShipMates is an 18+ platform, but we still expect mature, appropriate conduct. No explicit content, spam, or solicitation.

Stay Safe

Protect your personal information. Never share financial details, home addresses, or other sensitive information. Always meet in public places when connecting with other cruisers.

Be Honest

Use real photos and accurate information in your profile. Catfishing, impersonation, or misrepresentation is prohibited.

Report Issues

If you encounter inappropriate behavior, scams, or safety concerns, please report it immediately. We're committed to maintaining a safe community.

Consequences

Violations of these guidelines may result in warnings, temporary suspension, or permanent account termination depending on severity.

By using ShipMates, you agree to follow these community guidelines. Thank you for helping us create a positive environment for all cruisers!
